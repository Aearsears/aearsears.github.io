Ghost of Tsushima

⭐ 8/10 PC 25 hours played

Overview

Visually stunning open-world samurai adventure set in feudal Japan in the late 1200s during the Mongol invasion.

What I Loved

  • Beautiful open-world design and immersive atmosphere
  • Difficult but rewarding combat mechanics
  • Compelling story with well-developed characters

What Could Be Better

  • More variety in missions

Final Thoughts

I would recommend this game for fans of samurai and open-world games.
